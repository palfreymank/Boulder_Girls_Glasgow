What is Boulder Girls?

Boulder girls is a community for women who enjoy bouldering or want to give it a try. It's a friendly meet up ran by local Boulder Girls Emmy and Kirsty. We meet once a week and welcome all women of any ability.

Kirsty

Kirsty has been bouldering for over 2 years and has experience in both top rope climbing and bouldering indoors. You can often spot her at the Climbing Academy or the Climbing Centres, two of the best places for bouldering and climbing in Glasgow. Outside of bouldering. Kirsty works in education and she is also a keen runner and cyclist.

Emmy

Emmy has been actively climbing and bouldering for over two years now while her first climbing experiences were outdoors in the Bulgarian rocks. She regularly climbs in the the Climbing Academy and the Glasgow Climbing Centre and enjoys going there as often as she can! When Emmy is not climbing, she works in education and runs a food blog. She is also a keen cyclist though usually when the weather is nice.
